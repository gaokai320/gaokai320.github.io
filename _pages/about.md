I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Software Engineering at [School of Software Engineering and Microelectronics](http://ss.pku.edu.cn/) [Peking University](https://www.pku.edu.cn/), advised by [Minghui Zhou](http://sei.pku.edu.cn/~zhmh/) and [Bing Xie](http://sei.pku.edu.cn/~xiebing/) at [Open Source Software Data Analytics Lab](https://osslab-pku.github.io/). Before that, I received my Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science and Economy at Peking University in 2019. My research interests mainly focus on mining software repository and empirical studies for open-source software ecosystems and supply chain.
